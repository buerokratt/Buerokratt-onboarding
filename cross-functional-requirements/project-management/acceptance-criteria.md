## Bürokratt cross-functional requirements for acceptance criterias

> When talking about ACs in general, phrase `acceptance criterias` may be used.
> 
> When listing ACs as part of issue description, only phrase `Acceptance Criteria` is used.

### General requirements

- [ ] Acceptance Criteria is a mandatory part of every issue
- [ ] There are 3 types of acceptance criterias - for business requirements, GUI and DSL-based developments
- [ ] No developments may be started until acceptance criterias have been agreed upon and fixed as decisions by all related parties

### Acceptance Criteria for business requirements

- [ ] Created and confirmed by the Product Owner

### Acceptance Criteria for GUI developments

- [ ] Created and confirmed by Developers and Architects
- [ ] In case of disagreements, the final decision in made by the purchaser's Architect

### Acceptance Criteria for DSL-based developments

- [ ] Created and confirmed by Developers and Architects
- [ ] In case of disagreements, the final decision in made by the purchaser's Architect
